WHO ARE WE?

We are the Class of 2023 from the Medical Technology Department at Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS), Indonesia. One of our core missions is to innovate and develop accessible assistive technologies that break down barriers and improve the quality of life for people with disabilities.

SEE MORE

WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

We believe technology is the key to unlocking potential by breaking down barriers for people with disabilities in Indonesia, from mobility challenges to a lack of opportunities. Each product we create is designed to restore autonomy, improve well-being, and empower every individual to contribute fully to the nation's progress as we build a more equitable Indonesia for all.
